Blue Seal Rafting Company

Our mission is to deliver unforgettable whitewater rafting adventures that connect people with the natural beauty of rivers and the great outdoors. We are committed to providing thrilling, safe, and environmentally responsible experiences for individuals, families, and groups, while fostering a love for nature and outdoor activities.

History

Founded in 2000, Blue Seal Rafting Company began as a small, family and friends-run business offering guided whitewater rafting trips along the River Virginia. Over the years, the company grew in popularity due to its commitment to safety, experienced guides, and unforgettable adventure experiences. Today, it is a leading outdoor adventure company, recognized for sustainable practices and providing thrilling rafting excursions to thousands of guests annually.
